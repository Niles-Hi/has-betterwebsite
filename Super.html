Superintendent's Message

Dear HAS Community,
​
I am extremely excited about the 2016-2017 school year, as well as moving
forward in the future. Not only is this our 12th year, the school continues
to undergo steady improvement in the areas of instruction, extra-curricular
activities, and facilities. I am certain this will be one of the best years
in the history of the Hsinchu American School.
​
Our Principal, Mr. Anthony Donnelly, and Vice-Principal, Mrs. Lindsey Kruger,
create a dynamic duo of academic administrators who will oversee and facilitate
an excellent program for our students. We also have a professional and experienced
teaching staff to provide the best of instruction. Miss Sonia, along with our fine
office staff, will continue to provide excellent service to our parents and students
as well.

We are currently in the second year of a six-year WASC accreditation period and are
looking forward to our interim visit next spring. Gaining full accreditation was an
arduous process, but we are all proud of the outcome. Mr. Donnelly will be representing
our school both at EARCOS this fall and on a WASC visiting team to Mongolia in March.
I want to assure everyone that I am still here and will continue to work hard to reach
the goal that the Chairman, Mr. Yeh, and I established more than eight years ago: to create
the best international school in Taiwan and one of the best schools throughout all of Asia.

Let’s all work together to realize our school’s mission and vision.

Sincerely,
Darin L. Murphy
Superintendent/Head of School
