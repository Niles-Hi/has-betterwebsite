Interviews and Tour

You are welcome to come for an interview either before or after you complete the applicaition form.While
here for an interview, we encourage you to take a tour of the School. Seeing Hsinchu American School
students and faculty in action will encourage you to make a positive decision regarding attending the school.

Testing

For students applying to Hsinchu American School, a copy of standardized test results from the previous school
is needed in addition to transcripts. All candidates who speak a first language other than English will be given
a test to assess language proficiency. Test results will be reported to parents by either email or phone.
